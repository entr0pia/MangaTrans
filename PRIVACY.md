# Privacy Policy for MangaTrans

**Last Updated: March 15, 2026**

MangaTrans ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to ensuring your data privacy. This policy outlines how we handle information.

## 1. No Data Collection by Developer
MangaTrans **does not collect, store, or transmit** any of your personal identifiable information (PII) or browsing data to our own servers. We do not have access to your history, your settings, or the images you translate.

## 2. Local Data Storage
To provide its core functionality, the extension stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API:
- **API Credentials**: Your API keys and Base URL are saved in your browser to authenticate requests to your chosen AI provider.
- **User Preferences**: Settings such as target language and typography preferences are stored locally to personalize your experience.

## 3. Data Transmission to Third Parties
MangaTrans functions as a bridge between the website you are viewing and the **AI API provider** (e.g., Google Gemini, OpenAI, or other compatible services) that **you have manually configured**.
- **Manga Images**: When the "Auto-Translate" feature is active, the extension captures manga images from supported websites and sends them directly to your chosen API endpoint for OCR and translation.
- **Service Providers**: We encourage you to review the privacy policies of your chosen API provider to understand how they handle and process image data.

## 4. Security
All communications between the extension and your configured API endpoint are performed over encrypted **HTTPS** connections.

## 5. Contact
For any questions regarding this privacy policy or the extension's data practices, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository issues page.
